What makes Blockbot unique?

It's a smart block that integrates a robotics module within the shape of a block. You can freely create different configurations of BLOCKBOT to support creative play and learning activities for young children and elementary students.

Discover more

Blockbot is a brick-shaped, movable block

It can be assembled into any shape the user desires while also enabling robotic movement and programming.

Blockbot is a modular robot without cables

Instead of a fixed shape, it is provided as modular components, allowing for endless creativity. It can be assembled 100% wirelessly, eliminating the inconvenience of wired connections.

Blockbot connects to various computing devices

The patented connector of Blockbot structure facilitates power supply and data communication, removing redundancies in the architecture.
